The operating thesis

AI will not fix weak decision architecture. It will expose it.

As organizations add AI tools, agents, and automated workflows, the cost of unclear decisions rises. Before you automate a workflow, you need to understand the decision: its owner, inputs, constraints, risks, timing, and feedback loop.

Start with the decision, not the chart.
Name the tradeoff before building the metric.
Design for the operating rhythm, not the slide deck.
Build early wins that can become durable systems.
